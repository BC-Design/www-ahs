Welcome to BAMMM!

We're a bunch of people who are excited about applying the sane living philosophy championed by Mr. Money Mustache to life in the San Francisco Bay Area. We meet up semi-regularly to discuss topics in frugality, investing and personal finance, DIY culture, sustainability, education, community-building, and life in general. Join us when you're free and check it out!
